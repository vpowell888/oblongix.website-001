Privacy

At Oblongix, we take your privacy seriously. We're responsible for handling your personal data (referred to here as "we", "us", or "our").

We collect and process personal information when you visit our website, get in touch about our services, or sign up to receive marketing updates.

This privacy statement explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what your rights are.

The Data We Collect About You

We may collect, use, store, and share the following types of personal data:

  • Contact Data: such as your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Marketing and Communications Data: your preferences about receiving marketing from us and any third parties, if you've opted in.

How We Use Your Personal Data

We only use your personal data for the reasons we collected it. These include:

  • Responding to questions or requests you send us;
  • Managing and delivering our services;
  • Sending you updates about our services and job opportunities (only if you've opted in);
  • Improving how our website, services, and communications work for you.

How We Share Your Personal Data

We never sell your personal information. We may share it with trusted partners who help us run our business, but only when necessary and with appropriate safeguards in place.

Your Legal Rights

You have rights under data protection laws. These include the right to request a copy of your personal data and to make a complaint at any time to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), which regulates data protection. Visit www.ico.org.uk to learn more.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy statement or wish to exercise your rights, please email us at office@oblongix.com.
